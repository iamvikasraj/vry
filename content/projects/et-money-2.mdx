Design system work for ET Money — shared components and patterns across investing, insurance, and money management.

## Context

Placeholder case study — establishing a coherent UI language as ET Money scaled multiple product verticals.

## What we built

**Foundations** — type, color, spacing, and iconography aligned to brand.

**Components** — inputs, cards, charts, and navigation patterns for finance UIs.

**Documentation** — usage guidelines for design and engineering squads.

## Outcomes

Faster iteration on new features and more consistent member experiences across the app.
