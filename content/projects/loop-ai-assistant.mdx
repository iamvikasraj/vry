Loop’s general-query AI assistant helps members get fast answers without leaving the app — while keeping trust, clarity, and safe escalation to human care.

## The problem

Members had quick health questions but no lightweight way to get reliable answers inside Loop. Support and doctor flows were too heavy for “what does this mean?” moments.

## Approach

I designed the assistant around **progressive depth** — short answers first, optional detail, and clear paths to book a doctor or talk to support when the query needs a human.

**Conversation states** — loading, partial results, cited answers, and error recovery each have distinct treatment so members always know what the system is doing.

**SwiftUI prototyping** — built interactive prototypes on device to test timing, keyboard behavior, and handoff to care flows with engineering early.

## Tools

**SwiftUI** — native prototyping and production patterns  
**Figma** — flow mapping and conversation UI specs
