## India's first Rive Ambassador Happy Hour

What started as a casual evening at The Craftery by Subko turned into something I didn't fully expect — **40 designers, animators, and creative technologists** showed up, and almost no one wanted to leave.

I kept it unstructured on purpose. No talks, no agenda — just the local Rive community in one room. People who'd only ever known each other through social handles were finally meeting in person, and the conversations ran long: interactive design, careers, side projects, and where motion is headed.

It was India's **first** Rive Ambassador event — a **sold-out** evening officially hosted and sponsored by **Rive**, who published and promoted it themselves. The turnout made one thing clear: there's a real, hungry community here. More workshops are coming.

Thanks to Aditya Palkar and Raj Achari, and to everyone who stayed late.
