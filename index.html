About Me

Vikas Raj Yadav is a Staff Product Designer at Loop Health in Bengaluru, where he crafts intuitive and impactful digital health experiences. With expertise in interaction design, motion systems, and prototyping, Vikas brings ideas to life through detail-driven and human-centered design.

Previously, he led design teams at ETMoney, HDFC Bank, and Paytm, shaping products used by millions. His work spans FinTech interfaces, scalable design systems, and cross-platform experiences, with particular focus on motion and interaction detail. His portfolio showcases comprehensive case studies demonstrating expertise in user-centered design, system thinking, and innovative problem-solving across diverse digital platforms.

As one of the Rive and Play ambassadors from India, Vikas champions the future of interactive design. He is currently exploring designing and prototyping through code—especially with SwiftUI for mobile—pushing his craft beyond traditional design tools.
