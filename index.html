About me

Vikas Raj Yadav is a Design Director at Beem (US), specializing in creating intuitive digital experiences. With expertise in interaction design, motion systems, and prototyping, Vikas has previously led design teams at ETMoney, HDFC Bank, and Paytm.

Focused on the intersection of usability and aesthetics, his work spans FinTech interfaces, design systems, and cross-platform experiences, with particular attention to detail in motion and interaction.

Based in Gurgaon, India, Vikas is passionate about pushing the boundaries of digital design while ensuring experiences remain intuitive and accessible to users.
