Overview

I led the design of Paytm's "Buy Now Pay Later" feature, creating a secure and accessible credit solution for the platform's 6M+ active users. This project involved extensive user research, product thinking, and developing an intuitive interface that helped users understand their spending limits and manage payments effectively.

Key Challenges

Credit Bureau Gap

No traditional credit data for 6M+ users → Built alternative scoring model

User Experience

Poor limit distribution → Created intuitive bottle metaphor for credit visualization

Financial Literacy

New credit users → Added contextual help and simplified explanations

Design Evolution

Initial concept sketches exploring limit visualization

Refined wireframes with focus on available credit and spending limit

Information architecture for the BNPL feature integration

Visual metaphor exploration - "Bottle = Total Limit"

The Solution

Spend Limit Visualization

Created an intuitive visual representation of available and total credit limits, making it easy for users to understand their spending capacity at a glance.

User Education

Developed comprehensive FAQ sections and contextual help to educate users about the BNPL concept, payment schedules, and benefits.

Flexible Payment Options

Designed interfaces for early repayment, scheduled payments, and payment history to give users full control over their credit experience.
